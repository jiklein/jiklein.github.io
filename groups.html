Automated Receipt Management (ARM) (IEEE)

My IEEE Quarterly Projects team won 3rd place at our showcase!

We designed a utensil that helps stabilize tremors in patients with Parkinson's. Our device reads data from an IMU and uses servos to correct motion on two axis of rotation. It also sends the data to a website so that patients can track their sympotms over time.

I assisted in programing the core control algorithm in our device and designed all of the CAD for our prototype.
